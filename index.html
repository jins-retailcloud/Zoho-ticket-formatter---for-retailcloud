Retailcloud Zoho Ticket Formatter

How to use:

  1. Paste up to 10 tickets in the format shown below (separate tickets with an empty line).
  2. Click Generate & Copy. All tickets will be formatted and copied automatically.
  3. Hover over the Zoho ticket ID to see a tooltip: “Click to copy ticket metadata.”
  4. Paste the copied lines wherever needed (Slack, Docs, Emails).

Example format to paste:

Ticket ID: #114238
Subject: DODGERS | FW: Adjustment showing 0 QOH
Department: CS - General
URL: https://desk.zoho.com/agent/retail12/cs-general/tickets/details/594198000091620894
