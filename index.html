Zoho Ticket Formatter

How to use:
  1. In Zoho, hover over the ticket ID → click Copy Ticket Metadata
  2. Paste up to 10 tickets below (separate each with a blank line)
  3. Click Generate & Copy
  4. Paste directly into Slack / Email / Docs (links will be clickable)
Example input: 
Ticket ID: #114238
Subject: DODGERS | FW: Adjustment showing 0 QOH
Department: CS - General
URL: https://desk.zoho.com/agent/retail12/cs-general/tickets/details/594198000091620894
